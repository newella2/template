Communication Section

Actuaries communicate in a very odd way. They must be able to discuss information with their customers in a way that the customer will understand however also be able to use the language that all of the other actuaries use. In a quote form an article named "On Writing Actuarial Science" it says, "The first step in learning to be an actuary is to learn the jargon. The second step is to learn to use the jargon. The third is to forget the jargon." Jargon means the language so an actuary must learn the language then learns to use that language then forget all about that language and speak in normal english. This is part of what makes actuaries so special.
