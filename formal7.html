Conclusion

Overall, Actuarial Science is a growing industry that involves a lot of math mixed with communications and computer sciences. Figuring out how much money it will cost a company if a person decides to retire at 50 or if a person breaks his leg and has to take three months off of work how much money the company will lose there is not an easy job. Because not a lot of people like math or are very good at it these individuals receive quite a large sum of money given what they do and their special skills. They must do a lot and make some sacrifices but in the end some people still believe that it is worth it and want to pursue a career such as this.
