Introduction

In todays world everything has meaning behind the obvious. At first look Family Guy may just look like a funny show meant to make people laugh however in actuality it is a satirical show with story lines showing issues in todays culture. In the first episode of season 10 titled ”Lottery Fever” the issue that is brought about is how the life of a person changes once somebody wins the lottery. As soon as somebody wins the lottery everyone and their mother asks this person for money. I think this is just ridiculous. Well I think that anyone that wins the lottery should stay anonymous.
